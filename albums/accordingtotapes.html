according to tapes

according to tapes will be officially released on 10 of October 2023. It is the sixth album by Enjoy Everything While It Lasts and is the longest at 4 hours long. It will also not be on bandcamp due to some songs (e.g thats not me and a friendly follow up) for being to big in file size, the track "thats not me" is 1 gigabyte, bandcamp has a maximum set of megabytes for each song.
